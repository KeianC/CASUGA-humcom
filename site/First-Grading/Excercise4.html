Perspective Archetypes

What is a Perspective Art?

Linear or point-projection perspective is one of two types of graphical projection perspective in the graphic arts; the other is parallel projection. Linear perspective is an approximate representation, generally on a flat surface, of an image as it is seen by the eye. In linear perspective, there are 4 major types of perspective defined by the number of primary Vanishing Points lying on the Horizon Line:

  • 1-point perspective
  • 2-point perspective
  • 3-point perspective
  • Multi-point perspective.
